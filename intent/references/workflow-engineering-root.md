Procedure for **`docs/engineering/README.md`**. Template: [`../assets/engineering-root.md`](../assets/engineering-root.md). Conventions: [`../assets/CONVENTIONS.md`](../assets/CONVENTIONS.md).

## Draft session

1. **Load:** **B0** ([`../SKILL.md`](../SKILL.md) slice), **B1** (template + conventions), **B3** (product summary—root and/or outcome/requirement one-liners; avoid full trees unless necessary). Optional: [`context-loading.md`](context-loading.md).
2. **Draft** architecture/system view and **components table** with **links to product requirements**—authoritative mapping lives here only.
3. **Emit:** **CR** for meaningful edits; **ADR** under `docs/engineering/adrs/` for a **new** root-level architectural decision—[`emit-records.md`](emit-records.md).

## AI review session

1. **Load:** **B2** [`review-engineering.md`](review-engineering.md) (engineering root), **B1**, draft, **B3** (product summary).
2. **Incorporate** feedback; re-review if material changes.

## Second human review

**B2**, final README, one-screen product summary—[`context-loading.md`](context-loading.md).
