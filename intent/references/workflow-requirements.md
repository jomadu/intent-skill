# Workflow: Requirements

Procedure for **requirement one-liners / risks** on the outcome **`README.md`**, then **requirement detail** under **`outcomes/.../requirements/`**. Templates: [`../assets/requirement-simple.md`](../assets/requirement-simple.md), [`../assets/requirement-complex.md`](../assets/requirement-complex.md). Methodology: [`decomposing-requirements.md`](decomposing-requirements.md).

## Requirement index (tables on outcome README) — draft/review

1. **Load:** **B1** ([`../assets/outcome.md`](../assets/outcome.md)), **B3** (product root + outcome), **B4** (sibling requirements under other outcomes if checking cross-outcome conflicts).
2. **Decompose** using [`decomposing-requirements.md`](decomposing-requirements.md); lock one-liners before expanding files.
3. **Review:** **B2** [`review-product.md`](review-product.md) (requirements one-liners).

## Requirement detail — draft/review

1. **Load:** **B1** (simple or complex requirement template), **B3**, **B4**; optional minimal product trace (requirement IDs, component **names** only—not full mapping lists).
2. **Draft** capability, acceptance, edge cases; keep mapping in **`docs/engineering/README.md`** only.
3. **Review:** **B2** [`review-product.md`](review-product.md) (requirement artifact).

## Records

**CR** for meaningful edits—[`emit-records.md`](emit-records.md).
